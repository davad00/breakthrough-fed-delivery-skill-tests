Three Orbits of Practice

Stellar Meditation

First Orbit — Beginner

Breath and body as your first constellation. Learn to trace the outline of sensation, finding fixed points in the vastness of internal space. Each breath is a star — distant, steady, guiding.

Planetary Immersion

Second Orbit — Intermediate

Withdraw the senses inward, past the atmosphere of habit. Sound, sight, touch — all dissolve into the dark matter of deeper awareness. The body becomes a planet, self-contained, orbiting its own center.

Cosmic Absorption

Third Orbit — Advanced

Formless unity where observer and observed collapse into a single point of light. No boundary between self and space. The meditator becomes the void — infinite, luminous, still.
