The Path

A typical day at Oasis.

5:30 AM

Morning Bell

Tea is served in silence. The mountains emerge from darkness. A bell sounds, and sitting meditation begins.

7:00 AM

Movement

Yoga, qigong, or silent walking meditation. The body wakes before the mind.

9:00 AM

Breakfast

A plant-based meal prepared with intention, eaten in noble silence. Each ingredient chosen for its effect on the nervous system.

10:30 AM

Teaching & Inquiry

A senior teacher offers a dharma talk or guided inquiry. No dogma. Only direct investigation.

2:00 PM

Rest & Integration

Unstructured time for sleep, walking, or simply sitting with whatever arises. No agenda. No expectations.

7:30 PM

Evening Sit

The day closes with a final period of sitting, followed by the ringing of the bell. Silence holds the night.
