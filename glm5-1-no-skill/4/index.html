The Way

Between each breath lies an entire universe

Oasis is rooted in the principle that true luxury is the spaciousness to simply be. Our retreat draws from Zen contemplative tradition, refined over millennia and expressed through architecture, ritual, and silence.

We do not offer escape. We offer return — to the ground beneath your feet, to the rhythm of your own breath, to the quiet wisdom that lives in the pause between heartbeats.

Practice

Three Gates

Zazen

Seated meditation in our cedar-lined zendo. Morning and evening sessions guided by teachers with decades of practice. The foundation of all that follows.

Kinhin

Walking meditation through the forest paths — each step a return to the present moment. The boundary between meditation and life dissolves.

Chado

The way of tea as contemplative practice. Each bowl is an invitation to presence — to receive this moment fully, and let it pass.

Shinrin-yoku

Forest bathing along ancient trails. The pines and moss become your teachers, and the silence between trees becomes your shelter.

The Space

Architecture of emptiness

Our pavilions embody the principle of ma — the meaningful void. Every room breathes. Every view frames the wild. Nothing is excessive; nothing is missing.
