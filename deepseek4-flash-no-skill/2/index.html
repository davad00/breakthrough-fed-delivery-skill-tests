01
Approach

We do not teach mindfulness. We create the conditions for it to arise.

Our retreats are designed by meditation masters with over forty years of practice and a team of architects, chefs, and somatic therapists. Every detail — from the angle of a window to the temperature of the tea — is calibrated to support your return to presence.

02
Experiences

Four paths. One destination.

The Silent Week

Seven days of noble silence. No speaking, reading, or writing. Guided only by the sound of bells and the presence of a teacher who has not spoken in twelve years.

Breath & Body

Pranayama, cold exposure, and movement medicine. A systematic unwinding of stored tension through the body's own intelligence.

The Dream Temple

Yoga nidra, lucid dreaming protocols, and sleep hygiene. Six nights of guided deep rest in a chamber built specifically for the exploration of inner space.

Integration

Extended 21-day program combining plant medicine, depth psychology, and daily one-on-one mentorship. For those ready to meet what lies beneath.
