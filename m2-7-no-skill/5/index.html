"The sea is nothing but a collection of drops, yet without them, there would be no sea."

At Oasis, we gather these drops—individual souls seeking the same infinite horizon. Our coastal sanctuary offers a rare medicine: the combination of saltwater, silence, and the profound teaching that stillness and movement are not opposites, but dance partners in the eternal rhythm of existence.

The ancient Greeks had two words for time: chronos, the linear progression of seconds and years, and kairos, the qualitative experience of the right or fitting moment.


At Oasis, kairos is the only time that matters. We exist outside the tyranny of schedules. The tide determines the rhythm. The sunrise calls us to醒了. The moon invites us to surrender.

Where the Mountain Meets the Sea

Our sanctuary occupies a rare geography—the final ridge of the Andes descending directly into the Pacific. The setting creates a natural amphitheater where morning fog rolls in from the ocean and evening winds carry the scent of ancient cypress.

No roads visible from the property. No sounds of civilization. Only the eternal conversation between rock and wave.
