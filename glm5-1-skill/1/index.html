THE FOUNDATION OF OASIS

THREE PATHS INTO SILENCE

01

Silent Vipassana

Ten days of complete silence. No reading, no writing, no eye contact. The mind, stripped of every external stimulus, turns inward and discovers the landscape beneath thought. This is the original technology of transformation.

02

Sound Immersion

Binaural frequencies, singing bowls, and the resonance of a 400-year-old temple bell. Sound becomes architecture, building interior spaces the mind can inhabit. Each session is composed for the specific acoustics of our Kyoto chamber.

03

Movement Meditation

Slow-motion walking through the bamboo grove at dawn. Qigong under open sky. The body as meditation instrument, each movement a syllable in a language older than words. Guided by masters trained in lineages spanning twelve centuries.

Dissolve into the infinite.
