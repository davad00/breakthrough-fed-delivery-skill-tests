01 — Depth

"The mind is like water. When it is turbulent, it is difficult to see. When it is calm, everything becomes clear."

Oasis exists at the edge where California's granite cliffs fall into the Pacific. We built nothing until we understood what the land asked of us. The answer was space — radical, uncomfortable, clarifying space. Seven days on the California coast where the only obligation is to go under and come back changed.

Not a spa. Not a hotel. A laboratory for the interior life, where the ocean's indifference becomes the practitioner's greatest teacher.
