Four Paths

Each season, a new way to come home to yourself

Our programs follow the rhythm of the land. Arrive to olive groves and cypress-lined paths, and leave with a stillness that travels with you.

Verdant Stillness

Spring • 5 Days

Morning forest bathing, afternoon meditation in the olive grove, evenings with farm-to-table cuisine.

Golden Silence

Summer • 7 Days

Silent retreat under the Tuscan sun. Stone courtyard meditation and infinity pool contemplation.

Harvest Breath

Autumn • 5 Days

Pranayama among the vineyards. Grape harvest rituals and wine cellar sound baths at dusk.

Hearth Rest

Winter • 4 Days

Fireside meditation, thermal spring soaks, and slow mornings with hand-poured ceramics and tea.
