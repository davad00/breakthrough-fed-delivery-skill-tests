About

A stillness deeper than silence.

Nestled where the ocean meets ancient cliffs, Oasis is not a destination — it is a return. Our retreat is designed by masters of contemplative practice, architects of tranquility, and artisans of the sensory world. Every stone, every ripple, every breath of wind has been considered.

Retreats

Four paths. One stillness.

Silent Immersion — 7 days of absolute silence. Ocean Meditation — guided practice at the water's edge. Deep Rest — restorative yoga and sound bathing. Private Sanctuary — a bespoke retreat designed entirely around you.

Experience

Beyond meditation.

Float in our saltwater infinity pools suspended over the Pacific. Walk the stone labyrinths at dawn. Dine on cuisine cultivated from our own coastal gardens. Sleep in suites where the only sound is the rhythm of waves. This is05 not escape. This is arrival.
