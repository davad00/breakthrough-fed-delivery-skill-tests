Philosophy — Vol. IV

THE ART
OF DEEP
REST

We built Oasis on a radical premise: that genuine rest is not the absence of activity but the presence of awareness. That the body, given true silence, knows exactly how to heal itself. That luxury — real luxury — is nothing more than the careful removal of obstacles between a person and their own depth.

Founded in 2008 by neuroscientist Dr. Mara Solano and contemplative scholar Ren Asahi, Oasis occupies a 340-acre estate in the high desert. Every material decision, every schedule, every silence has been chosen to serve one purpose: the radical restoration of the human interior.

"The most subversive act in modern life is to sit completely still and pay attention to what arises."

— Dr. Mara Solano, Co-Founder

Our approach is empirical as much as experiential. Every retreat is preceded by a comprehensive neurological baseline assessment and followed with post-retreat data — cortisol panels, HRV readings, cognitive performance metrics — because we believe transformation should be measurable.

Process — Four Stages
01
Initial Contact
The Enquiry

Submit a brief written enquiry outlining your intentions. We read every submission personally. There is no automated system. A member of our curatorial team will respond within 72 hours.

02
Consultation
The Conversation

A sixty-minute call with one of our senior practitioners. We discuss your practice history, your nervous system, your intentions. This is how we understand what you need — and whether we are the right vessel for it.

03
Preparation
The Readying

Two weeks before arrival, you receive a preparation guide: dietary recommendations, a gentle preliminary practice, a list of what to bring — and what to leave behind. The retreat begins before you arrive.

04
Arrival
The Threshold

You are met at the gate by your personal guide. Devices are received with care. You cross into a different frequency. What happens next is yours alone to discover.

Begin the Process

APPLY
FOR A
STAY

We accept a maximum of 24 guests per season. Each application is reviewed individually. There are no waiting lists — only the right moment, which is always now.

Submit Enquiry Download Guide
  • 01
    Complete the Written Enquiry

    Three paragraphs. What brings you here. What you are carrying. What you are hoping to set down. We read between the lines.

  • 02
    Await Our Response

    Within 72 hours, a member of our team will reach out to schedule your consultation call. Response is not guaranteed — but sincerity always is noticed.

  • 03
    Complete the Practitioner Consultation

    A private 60-minute dialogue to assess fit, establish intentions, and begin the relationship that will shape your entire stay.

  • 04
    Receive Your Invitation

    If the timing and the fit are right, you will receive a formal letter of invitation — accompanied by your personal preparation guide and arrival instructions.

  • 05
    Confirm and Secure

    A modest deposit secures your place. The balance is due thirty days prior to arrival. Cancellation terms are humane — because life, unlike retreat, is unpredictable.
