Our Philosophy

Oasis was born from a conviction that the world does not need more productivity — it needs more presence. In 2019, after two decades of working in high-pressure environments, I stepped away and spent a year in silence, traversing mountain monasteries and coastal hermitages, learning what it truly means to rest. What I discovered was not an emptiness, but a profound fullness: that the most nourishing thing a human being can do is simply to be. Oasis is my offering back — twelve acres of unhurried landscape, warm stone and cedar, where guests arrive as seekers and leave as themselves.

Elena Vasquez
Founder & Lead Facilitator

The Oasis Experience

Felt in Every Sense

01

The Sound

A frequency of silence

Tibetan singing bowls at dawn. The creek that runs below the cedar grove. Wind in tall grass. We have designed Oasis so that silence is not empty — it is rich, layered, alive.

02

The Scent

Earth after warm rain

Our bespoke house blend — vetiver, sandalwood, and wild juniper — is diffused gently through common areas. Each morning, guests wake to fresh cedar and the faint sweetness of local wildflowers.

03

The Touch

Warmth in every texture

Hand-loomed linen bedding, stone floors that hold the heat of the sun, copper water vessels polished each morning. Our spaces are curated to remind the body that it is held.

04

The Taste

Food as ceremony

Our chef creates seasonal menus from what grows within ten miles. Meals are served slowly, eaten in silence or in gentle conversation — never rushed. Nourishment as a meditation of its own.

Things You May Wonder

Everything. Accommodation in your private cedar room, all meals and ceremonial teas, daily guided meditation sessions, optional one-to-one somatic sessions, nature walks, access to our bathhouse and library, and a small gift of our house scent blend to take home. The only thing we ask you to leave behind is the noise.
Not at all. Oasis welcomes complete beginners and seasoned practitioners alike. Our facilitators tailor each session to meet guests where they are. The only prerequisite is a genuine desire to slow down.
We host a maximum of twelve guests per retreat. This is a deliberate choice — intimacy and care are not possible at scale. Each guest receives genuine personal attention, and the group dynamic remains warm and unhurried throughout.
During Silent Immersion retreats, we kindly ask all guests to surrender their devices to our care team upon arrival. They are returned on your final morning. For Body & Breath and Grief & Renewal retreats, devices are permitted in your private room only, and we encourage mindful use.
We understand that life is unpredictable. Cancellations made 60 or more days before your retreat receive a full refund. Between 30–60 days, we offer a credit toward a future date. Within 30 days, we are unable to offer a refund but will always do our best to find an alternative solution.
Oasis is set within the high desert of northern New Mexico, two hours from Santa Fe. Upon booking, you will receive detailed arrival instructions and the option to arrange private transfer from Santa Fe Airport. We suggest arriving unhurried — the journey itself is part of the arrival.
