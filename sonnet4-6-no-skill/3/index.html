The Setting

A Living Sanctuary

Nestled within 40 acres of ancient temperate forest at 1,800 meters elevation, Oasis was conceived as a place where architecture disappears into landscape.

Each structure is built from reclaimed timber and local stone, oriented to capture morning light and evening stillness. No roads penetrate the inner sanctuary — guests arrive by a candlelit forest path.
