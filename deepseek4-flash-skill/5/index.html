/// The Oracle Speaks

The Oracle

Words are the first form of meditation. The right sentence at the right moment can rewire a lifetime of noise.

Hear the Oracle Read the Verses
/// Verse I
You are not your
thoughts.
You are the one
watching them.

The first realization. The observer and the observed are not the same. Between them lies the entirety of the practice.

/// Verse II
Stillness is not the absence of motion

It is motion so refined that it becomes invisible. Like the rotation of the earth. Or the beating of your heart when you finally stop listening for it.

/// Verse III
You have been
searching
for what you
already have.

The retreat does not give you anything new. It removes everything that hides what has always been there.

/// Verse IV
Breathe until the breath breathes you

This is the inversion at the heart of all practice. You do not meditate. Meditation happens. You only have to get out of its way.

The oracle is
within

Three days. Twelve guests. One practice.
